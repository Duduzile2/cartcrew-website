What We're Building

Planned Features

Built for how South Africans actually shop

01

Instant Price Comparison

Search any grocery product and instantly see prices across all 5 major SA retailers side by side. Know exactly where to shop before you leave home — every time.

02

Shared Family Baskets

Build a basket together as a family. Share it to WhatsApp with one tap. Everyone adds their items. CartCrew finds the cheapest single store for your whole week's shop.

03

WhatsApp Price Alerts

Track any product and receive a WhatsApp message the moment the price drops below your target. No push notifications. No checking. Just a message when it matters.

04

Community Deals Board

Spotted a special in store? Share it with the CartCrew community. Browse deals your neighbours have flagged nearby. Real deals from real people in real time.

05

Price History Graphs

See exactly how any product's price has moved over the past 3 months. Know whether that "special" is genuinely a deal — or just the regular price with a sticker on it.

06

Mobile App — Coming Soon

CartCrew launches on web first — then comes to Android and iOS. No download needed to start. The full app experience is on its way for both platforms.
